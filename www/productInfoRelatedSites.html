Open Source is part of the DNA of Optaros. Optaros uses Open Source components themselves and on their client engagements. They actively contribute to existing open source projects and when they find client needs that aren't being addressed they may also publish and maintain their own projects that can be found here on code.optaros.com.
Alfresco is the Open Source Alternative for Enterprise Content Management (ECM), providing Document Management, Collaboration, Records Management, Knowledge Management, Web Content Management and Imaging.
SourceForge.net is the world's largest Open Source software development web site, hosting more than 100.000 projects and over 1.000.000 registered users with a centralized resource for managing projects, issues, communications, and code.
